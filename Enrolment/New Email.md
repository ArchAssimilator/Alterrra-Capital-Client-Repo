## OBSIDIAN & MARKDOWN

During the Masterclass, we will be showing/teaching/encouraging you to use Markdown as much as possible in your AI workflows. In light of that, we recommend installing the application Obsidian:

**Obsidian**

[https://obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md)

The desktop app (for MacOS and Windows) can be downloaded from: [https://obsidian.md/download](https://obsidian.md/download)

Mobile apps can be downloaded for iOS and Android. Obsidian is a free Markdown reader and note-taking app that does not require an account.

**DON'T ATTEMPT TO SET UP AN ACCOUNT OR VAULT FOR THIS APPLICATION (THAT IS NOT NECESSARY); WE WILL DEMONSTRATE HOW TO USE IT PROPERLY!**

That being said, both Markdown and Obsidian are optional — neither is necessary for the successful completion of the Masterclass.

In previous Masterclasses, we found that Markdown and Obsidian can seem counter-intuitive, predominantly because they are so simple. We've therefore created a short briefing document (attached as `markdown-obsidian-guide.md`) explaining why we recommend them — please read it before the start of the Masterclass.
