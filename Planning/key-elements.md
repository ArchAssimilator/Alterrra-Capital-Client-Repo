# Key Elements for Alterra Capital AI Training Course

## Audience Profile & Level-Setting

- Participants range in AI experience; a **pre-reading pack** covering basic AI concepts should be distributed beforehand
- Core training pitched at **medium-to-advanced level**, avoiding time spent on fundamentals already covered in pre-reading
- Brief opening level-set to ensure no one is left behind before moving to applied content
- Optimal group size: **up to 12 participants**

## Module 1: AI Foundations (Condensed)

- AI model context windows, memory features, and how they affect output quality
- **Context rot**: what it is and how to avoid it through prompt structuring
- Managing project-specific memory and settings across sessions
- Tool landscape overview: strengths of ChatGPT (industry research, up-to-date data), Claude (reasoning, long documents), and Gemini — when to use which

## Module 2: Prompt Engineering

- Structuring prompts for reliable, repeatable outputs
- Building reusable prompt libraries (directly applicable to capital raising Q&A)
- Best practices for managing context and providing reference material to AI models

## Module 3: Deep Research

- Using AI for in-depth analysis of lengthy opportunity documents
- Extracting and summarising key information from investment memoranda and financial reports
- Limitations and workarounds for PDF-to-data extraction workflows

## Module 4: Agent-Based Automation

- Introduction to AI agents and agentic workflows
- Automating multi-step processes end-to-end
- Integration considerations with existing tools (Microsoft ecosystem, Excel, PowerPoint)

## Key Use Cases to Build Exercises Around

### 1. Capital Raising Automation
- Auto-generating answers to predictable investor questions from a pre-built Q&A library
- Automating PowerPoint preparation for investor meetings
- Meeting scheduling and follow-up workflows

### 2. Opportunity Evaluation Pipeline
- Processing lengthy funding applications/opportunity documents
- Extracting financials and generating initial investment committee presentations
- Moving from ChatGPT-generated drafts to integrated PowerPoint outputs

### 3. Legal Document Review
- AI-assisted review and summarisation of NDAs and contracts
- Automating initial legal review before external counsel involvement
- Negotiation point extraction and tracking

### 4. Portfolio Company Quarterly Reporting
- Extracting financial data from portfolio company reports (PDF to Excel)
- Reformatting and validating data against Alterra's templates
- Overlaying Alterra's own assumptions on extracted data
- Reducing junior staff time on repetitive data handling

### 5. Back/Middle Office Operations
- Invoice processing and payment workflows
- Travel expense automation
- HR task automation

## Group Projects (Hands-On)

- Real Alterra use cases should drive group project work
- Priority candidates: **quarterly reporting automation** and **NDA review**
- Projects should produce directly applicable, reusable outputs participants can take back to their work

## Vibe Coding Session

- Include an engaging, creative coding exercise to boost energy and interest
- Balance between fun/exploratory and business-relevant outcomes
- Serves as an accessible entry point to building with AI

## Technical Challenges to Address in Training

- Limitations of AI with complex, multi-dimensional spreadsheets (requires intermediate steps and data reformatting)
- Manual PDF-to-Excel workflows and where AI can/cannot yet fully automate
- Microsoft product integration gaps and current workarounds
- Setting realistic expectations about what AI can reliably handle today

## Course Format

- **Two-day programme** (in-person)
- Modular structure with flexibility to adapt based on participant needs
- Mix of instruction, demonstration, and hands-on project work
- Locations: Nairobi (primary), Johannesburg (secondary)

## Pre-Course Requirements

- Example quarterly reports from Alterra (preferably from exited/rejected companies)
- Typical PDF funding application for use in exercises
- Participant list with roles and AI experience levels for tailoring
