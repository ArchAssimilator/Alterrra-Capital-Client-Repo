# JSE-Listed Analogues to Alterra Portfolio Companies

The list below includes **only companies that are currently listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange (JSE)**.  
These companies act as **business-model or sector proxies** for companies Alterra Capital has invested in or been associated with.

---

# Consumer & Food Platforms

### Java House (Alterra investment)
Closest JSE-listed comparables:

- **Famous Brands (JSE: FBR)**  
  Vertically integrated restaurant franchisor with manufacturing and logistics capabilities.

- **Spur Corporation (JSE: SUR)**  
  Multi-brand casual dining restaurant franchisor with a strong African footprint.

These are the best listed proxies for a scalable African restaurant platform with franchise economics.

---

### Chill Beverages (Alterra investment)
Closest JSE-listed comparables:

- **AVI (JSE: AVI)** – diversified branded food and beverage manufacturer.
- **Tiger Brands (JSE: TBS)** – large FMCG manufacturer with extensive food brands.
- **Premier Group (JSE: PMR)** – major South African food manufacturing and distribution platform.

Together these companies represent the **closest public-market reference set for a branded beverage / FMCG distribution business**.
